\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Abelian group formed by integers under the action of addition. Useful for particles and spin conservation (along the quantization axis only). In principle \mbox{\hyperlink{classquantit_1_1conserved_1_1Z}{Z}} has an infinite domain, but here it is limited to \mbox{[}-\/32767,32767\mbox{]} by usage of int16\+\_\+t in the implementation

This group can represent the conservation of the charges associated with U1 symmetries

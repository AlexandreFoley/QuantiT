Detailed Description

Abelian group formed by integers under the action of addition. Useful for particles and spin conservation (along the quantization axis only). In principle Z has an infinite domain, but here it is limited to [-32767,32767] by usage of int16_t in the implementation

This group can represent the conservation of the charges associated with U1 symmetries
